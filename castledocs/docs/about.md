# About CASTLE
The CASTLE Lab weekly meetings are a place for students who are interested in computing related topics to socialize and share knowledge outside of the classroom, and to join extracurricular projects and events related to computing (and computer science). The CASTLE Lab is also open for students to use during the day as a social and work space. We hope to see you at our next meeting!

## Acronym
CASTLE stands for the Computer And Software Teaching-Learning Experience.
